## 👔 Dress Code
**Professional Casual to Party Attire – Your Choice!**
This is a 6 PM to Midnight event combining serious business with networking energy. Wear what makes you feel confident and comfortable. 

**Suggested Styles:**
* **Professional:** Business casual (smart shirts, trousers, blazers), startup founder look (jeans + blazer), or corporate professional (if coming from the office).
* **Party/Networking:** Smart casuals, evening wear, creative/tech industry casual.

> **Bottom Line:** Whether you show up in a suit or a hoodie, as long as you're here to build, learn, or invest - you're dressed right.
> **Not Recommended:** Gym wear, sleepwear, or overly casual beach attire.
