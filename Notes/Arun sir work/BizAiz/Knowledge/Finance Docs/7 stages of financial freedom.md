**Here are the **7 stages of financial freedom**, where "grocery purchase freedom" (buying basics without stress) typically falls around **Stage 3–4**. The higher stages above it are:

## The 7 Stages

|#|Stage|What It Means|
|---|---|---|
|1|**Dependence**|Rely on others to cover expenses [bankwithchoice](https://bankwithchoice.com/wealth-blog/the-different-stages-of-financial-freedom/)|
|2|**Solvency**|Income covers bills; no reliance on others [spaceship](https://www.spaceship.com.au/learn/the-seven-stages-of-financial-freedom/)|
|3|**Breathing Room**|Surplus after expenses; can save & invest [finance.yahoo](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/7-stages-financial-freedom-climb-183005409.html)|
|4|**Security**|Debt-free, emergency fund, can weather job loss [tdecu](https://www.tdecu.org/wealth-advisors-blog/the-different-stages-of-financial-freedom)|
|5|**Flexibility**|2+ years of lifestyle covered by savings; can quit job or take sabbatical [segurosypensionesparatodos.fundacionmapfre](https://segurosypensionesparatodos.fundacionmapfre.org/en/blog/stages-of-financial-freedom/)|
|6|**Independence**|Passive/investment income covers your entire lifestyle — you don't _need_ to work [reddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/financialindependence/comments/4c15ke/the_six_stages_of_financial_freedom/)|
|7|**Abundance**|Wealth far exceeds needs; can build legacy, charity, or anything you want [finance.yahoo](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/7-stages-financial-freedom-climb-183005409.html)|

## Stages Above "Grocery Freedom"

If you're at the grocery-purchase-without-stress stage (~Stage 3), the key milestones ahead are:

- **Security (4):** Build 6–12 months emergency fund, eliminate all debt[tdecu](https://www.tdecu.org/wealth-advisors-blog/the-different-stages-of-financial-freedom)
    
- **Flexibility (5):** Accumulate 2+ years of expenses in savings/investments[segurosypensionesparatodos.fundacionmapfre](https://segurosypensionesparatodos.fundacionmapfre.org/en/blog/stages-of-financial-freedom/)
    
- **Independence (6):** Grow investments until passive income = living expenses (classic FIRE goal)[reddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/financialindependence/comments/4c15ke/the_six_stages_of_financial_freedom/)
    
- **Abundance (7):** Passive income greatly exceeds needs; leave a financial legacy[finance.yahoo](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/7-stages-financial-freedom-climb-183005409.html)**
