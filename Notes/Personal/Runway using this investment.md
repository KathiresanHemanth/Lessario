initial_amount = 500000
one_time_deduction = 150000
monthly_loan = 15000
monthly_rent = 12000
monthly_food_travel = 15000
monthly_pc_rent = 10000

remaining_balance = initial_amount - one_time_deduction
total_monthly_expenses = monthly_loan + monthly_rent + monthly_food_travel + monthly_pc_rent
runway_months = remaining_balance / total_monthly_expenses

print(f"{remaining_balance=}")
print(f"{total_monthly_expenses=}")
print(f"{runway_months=}")

To calculate your runway, we first determine your remaining balance after the upfront payment and then divide that by your total monthly recurring expenses.

### **1. Calculation Breakdown**

- **Initial Amount:** $₹5,00,000$ (5L)
    
- **Upfront Deduction (Loan Repay):** $- ₹1,50,000$ (1.5L)
    
- **Remaining Balance:** **$₹3,50,000$**
    

### **2. Monthly Recurring Expenses**

- **Loan Repay:** $₹15,000$
    
- **House Rent:** $₹12,000$
    
- **Food & Travel:** $₹15,000$
    
- **PC Rent:** $₹10,000$
    
- **Total Monthly Expense:** **$₹52,000$**
    

---

### **3. The Runway**

$$\text{Runway} = \frac{\text{Remaining Balance}}{\text{Monthly Expenses}} = \frac{3,50,000}{52,000}$$

**Your runway is approximately 6.73 months.**

In practical terms, you have enough funds to last for **6 months and about 22 days**, assuming no other income or unexpected expenses occur.
