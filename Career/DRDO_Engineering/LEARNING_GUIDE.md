# DRDO Engineering - Learning Guide

## 🎯 Goal
Prepare for defense research careers — covering hardware electronics, embedded software, and military vehicle systems under DRDO (Defence Research and Development Organisation).

---

## Prerequisites
✅ Programming — C/C++ (embedded), Python, MATLAB
✅ Electronics — Analog/Digital circuits, microcontrollers
✅ Physics — Electromagnetics, Mechanics, Optics
✅ Mathematics — Signals & Systems, Linear Algebra, Probability

---

## Three Specialization Tracks

### Track 1: Hardware (Electronics & PCB)
1. **Basic Electronics** — Resistors, capacitors, transistors, op-amps
2. **Digital Logic** — Gates, flip-flops, counters, FSM
3. **Microcontrollers** — 8051, AVR, ARM (STM32)
4. **PCB Design** — Schematic capture, layout, routing, DFM
5. **Sensor Systems** — Radar, sonar, IR, thermal imaging
6. **RF & Communication** — Antenna design, modulation, SDR
7. **Power Electronics** — Converters, inverters, motor drives
8. **FPGA** — Verilog/VHDL, digital design, high-speed processing

### Track 2: Software (Embedded & Control)
1. **Embedded C** — Register-level programming, bare metal
2. **RTOS** — FreeRTOS, task scheduling, semaphores, mutexes
3. **Communication Protocols** — UART, SPI, I2C, CAN, Ethernet
4. **Control Algorithms** — PID, Kalman filter, state estimation
5. **Signal Processing** — DSP, FFT, filtering, noise reduction
6. **Cybersecurity** — Encryption, secure boot, tamper detection
7. **Real-time Systems** — Deterministic timing, interrupt handling
8. **Testing & Verification** — Unit testing, HIL, SIL

### Track 3: Automobile (Military Vehicles)
1. **Military Vehicle Design** — Armored vehicles, MRAPs, tanks
2. **Powertrain** — Heavy-duty engines, hybrid-electric drives
3. **Armor & Protection** — Ballistic protection, mine resistance
4. **NBC Protection** — Nuclear, biological, chemical defense
5. **Communication Systems** — Tactical radio, encrypted comms
6. **Navigation** — INS/GPS, terrain navigation, dead reckoning
7. **Weapon Integration** — Turret systems, fire control
8. **Autonomous Operations** — UGVs, remote operation

## DRDO Career Path

### How to Join DRDO:
1. **GATE Score** → Apply through DRDO RAC (Recruitment & Assessment Centre)
2. **SET Exam** → DRDO Scientist Entry Test (for Scientist 'B')
3. **Campus Placement** → Select IITs/NITs
4. **Apprenticeship** → DRDO labs accept apprentices

### Key DRDO Labs:
| Lab | Focus |
|-----|-------|
| **DRDL** (Hyderabad) | Missiles & Strategic Systems |
| **LRDE** (Bangalore) | Radar & Electronics |
| **CABS** (Bangalore) | Airborne Systems |
| **CVRDE** (Chennai) | Combat Vehicles |
| **RCI** (Hyderabad) | Research Centre Imarat |
| **DEAL** (Dehradun) | Electronics & Avionics |
| **HEMRL** (Pune) | High Energy Materials |

### Salary (Scientist 'B'):
- Basic Pay: ₹56,100/month (7th Pay Commission)
- With allowances: ~₹80,000-1,00,000/month
- Additional: HRA, DA, research allowance

- Clear GATE with high score (ECE/EE/ME/CS)
- Focus on embedded systems and signal processing
- Build projects with STM32 and FreeRTOS
- Learn defense-specific protocols and standards
- Security clearance is required — maintain clean record
- Publish papers if targeting Scientist roles
