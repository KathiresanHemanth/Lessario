## Core Concepts

1. **Battery Chemistry** — Li-ion, LFP, NMC, solid-state, sodium-ion
2. **Cell Design** — Cylindrical, pouch, prismatic, electrode structure
3. **Battery Management System (BMS)** — SOC, SOH, cell balancing, thermal
4. **Charging** — CC-CV, fast charging, wireless charging, standards
5. **Battery Pack Design** — Series/parallel, thermal management, safety
6. **Solar Photovoltaics** — PV cells, modules, inverters, MPPT
7. **Energy Storage Systems** — Grid-scale batteries, pumped hydro, hydrogen
8. **Power Electronics** — DC-DC converters, inverters, motor drives
9. **Renewable Grid Integration** — Smart grid, demand response, microgrids
10. **Hydrogen & Fuel Cells** — Electrolysis, PEM fuel cells, storage

## Key Formulas
```
Battery Capacity (Wh) = Voltage (V) × Capacity (Ah)
C-Rate: 1C = full charge/discharge in 1 hour
Energy Density = Wh / kg (gravimetric) or Wh / L (volumetric)
Solar Panel Output = Area × Efficiency × Irradiance (1000 W/m²)
```
