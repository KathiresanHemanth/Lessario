# Civil Engineering - Learning Guide

## 🎯 Goal
Understand structural design, construction, and infrastructure — from materials to large-scale project management.

---

## Prerequisites
✅ Physics — Mechanics, Statics, Strength of Materials
✅ Mathematics — Calculus, Geometry, Trigonometry
✅ Drawing — Engineering drawing, AutoCAD basics

---

## Core Concepts (in order)

1. **Engineering Mechanics** — Forces, moments, equilibrium, free body diagrams
2. **Strength of Materials** — Stress, strain, bending, shear, torsion
3. **Structural Analysis** — Beams, trusses, frames, deflection
4. **Concrete Technology** — Mix design, curing, reinforcement
5. **Steel Design** — Connections, columns, beams, IS codes
6. **Soil Mechanics** — Soil classification, bearing capacity, settlement
7. **Fluid Mechanics** — Bernoulli's equation, pipe flow, open channels
8. **Surveying** — Total station, GPS, leveling, contouring
9. **Construction Planning** — CPM, PERT, Gantt charts, estimation
10. **Environmental Engineering** — Water treatment, sewage, waste management
11. **Transportation Engineering** — Road design, traffic flow, highway geometry

---

## Key Formulas

### Structural
```
Stress (σ) = Force / Area
Strain (ε) = Change in Length / Original Length
Young's Modulus (E) = Stress / Strain
Bending Stress = M × y / I
Shear Stress = V × Q / (I × b)
```

### Concrete
```
Water-Cement Ratio → w/c = Water / Cement (by weight)
Compressive Strength → test at 7 and 28 days
Mix Design → M20 = 1:1.5:3 (Cement:Sand:Aggregate)
```

## Indian Context — Important Codes
- **IS 456** — Plain and reinforced concrete
- **IS 800** — Steel structures
- **IS 875** — Design loads
- **IS 1893** — Earthquake resistant design
- **IS 13920** — Ductile detailing for RC structures
- **IRC codes** — Road and bridge design

---

## Tips
- Master AutoCAD and STAAD Pro — they're industry essentials
- Learn IS codes thoroughly for Indian practice
- Site visits are invaluable — see theory in practice
- Prepare for GATE if targeting PSUs (NHPC, NHAI, CPWD)
- Quantity estimation skills are always in demand

Build strong foundations! 🏗️
