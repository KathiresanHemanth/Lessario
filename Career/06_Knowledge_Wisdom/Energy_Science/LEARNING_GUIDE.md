# Energy Science - Complete Guide

## 🎯 Goal
Understand energy at every level — from atoms to stars, from batteries to black holes. Energy is the single most fundamental concept in all of science and engineering.

## What IS Energy?

```
Energy = The capacity to do work or cause change.

It cannot be created or destroyed — only transformed.
(First Law of Thermodynamics)

Everything in the universe IS energy.
- Matter is frozen energy (E = mc²)
- Light is electromagnetic energy
- Heat is kinetic energy of atoms
- Sound is mechanical wave energy
- Your thoughts are electrochemical energy
- Stars are nuclear fusion energy
```

---

## The 10 Forms of Energy

### 1. Kinetic Energy (Motion)
```
E = ½mv²
A car at 100 km/h, a spinning turbine, wind, flowing water
Everything that moves has kinetic energy
```

### 2. Potential Energy (Stored)
```
Gravitational: E = mgh (object above ground)
Elastic: E = ½kx² (compressed spring, pulled bow)
A ball at the top of a hill, water behind a dam
```

### 3. Thermal Energy (Heat)
```
Random motion of atoms and molecules
Faster vibration = higher temperature
Fire, friction, sun's warmth, body heat
Heat always flows from hot → cold (2nd Law of Thermo)
```

### 4. Chemical Energy (Bonds)
```
Stored in chemical bonds between atoms
Released during chemical reactions

Examples:
- Food → body burns it for ATP (biological energy)
- Gasoline → engine burns it for motion
- Battery → electrochemical reaction → electricity
- Wood → fire → heat + light
```

### 5. Electrical Energy (Electrons)
```
Flow of electrons through a conductor
P = V × I (Power = Voltage × Current)

Lightning bolt = 1-5 billion joules
Your phone charger = ~5 watts
Tesla Powerwall = 13.5 kWh stored
Power plant = 500-1000 MW
```

### 6. Nuclear Energy (Atoms)
```
E = mc²

Fission: Split heavy atoms (uranium) → massive energy
  - Nuclear power plants, atomic bombs
  - 1 kg uranium = energy of 2,500 TONS of coal

Fusion: Combine light atoms (hydrogen → helium)
  - How stars work (including our Sun)
  - ITER reactor (being built in France)
  - 1 kg hydrogen fusion = 4x more than fission
  - "Unlimited clean energy" — holy grail of humanity
```

### 7. Electromagnetic Energy (Light)
```
Photons — particles/waves of electromagnetic radiation

Spectrum (low → high energy):
Radio → Microwave → Infrared → Visible → UV → X-ray → Gamma

E = hf (Energy = Planck's constant × frequency)

Solar panels convert photons → electricity
WiFi/Bluetooth = radio waves = low-energy EM radiation
X-rays = high-energy EM → can penetrate body
```

### 8. Sound Energy (Vibrations)
```
Mechanical waves through a medium (air, water, solid)
Speed of sound in air: 343 m/s
Cannot travel through vacuum (no medium)
Loudness measured in decibels (dB)
Whisper = 30 dB, Concert = 120 dB, Rocket launch = 180 dB
```

### 9. Gravitational Energy
```
Every mass attracts every other mass
F = GmM/r²

Earth's gravity holds atmosphere, oceans, YOU
Moon's gravity causes tides
Sun's gravity holds solar system together
Black holes = extreme gravitational energy (warps spacetime)
```

### 10. Dark Energy (The Mystery)
```
68% of the universe is dark energy
We don't know what it is
It's accelerating the expansion of the universe
Discovered in 1998 (Nobel Prize 2011)
The biggest unsolved mystery in physics
```

## The Laws of Thermodynamics

### 0th Law: Thermal Equilibrium
```
If A is in thermal equilibrium with C,
and B is in thermal equilibrium with C,
then A and B are in thermal equilibrium.
(This is why thermometers work)
```

### 1st Law: Conservation of Energy
```
Energy cannot be created or destroyed, only transformed.
ΔU = Q - W
(Change in internal energy = Heat added - Work done)

This is WHY perpetual motion machines are impossible.
```

### 2nd Law: Entropy Always Increases
```
In any natural process, total entropy (disorder) increases.
Heat flows from hot → cold, never reverse.
You can't un-scramble an egg.
Stars burn out. Buildings decay. Everything tends toward disorder.

This is the "arrow of time" — why time moves forward.
```

### 3rd Law: Absolute Zero
```
As temperature approaches absolute zero (0 K = -273.15°C),
entropy approaches zero.

You can never actually REACH absolute zero.
Closest achieved: 38 picokelvin (38 trillionths of a degree above 0)
```
