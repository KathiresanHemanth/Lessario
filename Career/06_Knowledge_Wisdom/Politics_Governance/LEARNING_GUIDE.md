## 🇮🇳 PART 2: Indian Politics

### Government Structure
```
INDIA — Federal Parliamentary Democratic Republic

CENTRAL GOVERNMENT (New Delhi)
├── President — Head of State (ceremonial)
├── Prime Minister — Head of Government (real power)
├── Parliament (Bicameral)
│   ├── Lok Sabha — 543 elected MPs (Lower House, more powerful)
│   └── Rajya Sabha — 245 members (Upper House, state representation)
├── Judiciary
│   ├── Supreme Court — Chief Justice + judges
│   ├── High Courts — One per state
│   └── District Courts — Local level
└── Executive — PM + Council of Ministers + Bureaucracy (IAS/IPS)

STATE GOVERNMENT
├── Governor — Appointed by President
├── Chief Minister — Elected (real power)
├── State Legislature
│   ├── Legislative Assembly (Vidhan Sabha) — Elected MLAs
│   └── Legislative Council (Vidhan Parishad) — Only in some states
└── State Bureaucracy — IAS officers as District Collectors
```

### How Indian Government Works (For Entrepreneurs)
```
To start a company:
1. Incorporate on MCA portal (Ministry of Corporate Affairs)
2. Get GST registration
3. Register on Startup India (DPIIT) for benefits
4. Open current account in bank
5. Comply: GST filing, TDS, ROC annual returns

Government bodies you'll interact with:
- MCA — Company registration
- GST Council — Tax
- SEBI — If raising public money
- RBI — Banking/payments regulation
- DPIIT — Startup recognition
- MEITY — Tech policy
```

## 🏛️ PART 3: Tamil Nadu Politics

### Political Landscape
```
TAMIL NADU — Dravidian politics dominates since 1967

Key History:
1916 — Justice Party (anti-Brahmin movement)
1944 — Periyar founds Dravidar Kazhagam (DK)
1949 — C.N. Annadurai breaks away, forms DMK
1967 — DMK wins, ends Congress rule in TN forever
1972 — MGR breaks away, forms AIADMK
1977-87 — MGR as CM (extremely popular)
1991-2016 — Jayalalithaa era (AIADMK dominance)
2021-present — DMK under M.K. Stalin wins majority
```

## 🏘️ PART 5: Virudhunagar District

### Overview
```
District: Virudhunagar (carved from Ramanathapuram in 1985)
Headquarters: Virudhunagar town
Population: ~19.4 lakh (2011 census)
Area: 4,283 sq km
Language: Tamil
Known As: "Fireworks Capital of India"

Taluks: Virudhunagar, Sivakasi, Aruppukkottai, Srivilliputhur,
        Sattur, Rajapalayam, Tiruchuli, Watrap

Major Towns:
- Sivakasi — Fireworks, printing, matchbox industry
- Rajapalayam — Textiles, Rajapalayam dog breed
- Srivilliputhur — Famous Andal temple, palkova (sweet)
- Aruppukkottai — Cotton ginning, trade center
- Sattur — Agriculture, small industries
```
