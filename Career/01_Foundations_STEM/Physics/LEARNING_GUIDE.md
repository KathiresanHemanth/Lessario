## Learning Path

### Phase 1: Classical Mechanics (Week 1-4)
1. **Kinematics** — Position, velocity, acceleration, projectile motion
2. **Newton's Laws** — F=ma, friction, normal force, tension
3. **Work & Energy** — KE, PE, conservation, power
4. **Momentum** — Conservation, collisions, impulse
5. **Rotational Mechanics** — Torque, angular momentum, moment of inertia

### Phase 2: Waves, Optics & Thermo (Week 5-8)
6. **Oscillations** — SHM, pendulum, resonance, damping
7. **Waves** — Sound, light, interference, diffraction
8. **Thermodynamics** — Laws, heat engines, entropy, cycles
9. **Optics** — Reflection, refraction, lenses, lasers

### Phase 3: Electromagnetism (Week 9-12)
10. **Electrostatics** — Coulomb's law, electric field, potential
11. **Current & Circuits** — Ohm's law, Kirchhoff, RC/RL/RLC
12. **Magnetism** — Magnetic field, force, induction, Faraday
13. **Electromagnetic Waves** — Maxwell's equations, spectrum

### Phase 4: Modern Physics (Week 13-16)
14. **Quantum Mechanics** — Wave-particle duality, Schrödinger
15. **Nuclear Physics** — Fission, fusion, radioactivity
16. **Solid State Physics** — Band theory, semiconductors, conductors
17. **Relativity** — Special relativity, E=mc², time dilation

## First Principles Thinking (Elon Musk's Method)
```
Traditional thinking: "Batteries cost $600/kWh, that's expensive"

First principles:
1. What are batteries made of? (Cobalt, nickel, lithium, carbon, steel)
2. What do these materials cost on London Metal Exchange?
3. Answer: ~$80/kWh in raw materials
4. Therefore: Build a factory and make them yourself
→ Tesla Gigafactory was born
```

**Apply this to EVERY problem:**
- Don't ask "how is it done?" → Ask "WHY is it done this way?"
- Break down to fundamental truths
- Rebuild from ground up

## Key Equations Everyone Should Know
```
F = ma                           (Newton's Second Law)
E = mc²                          (Mass-Energy Equivalence)
E = ½mv²                         (Kinetic Energy)
F = -kx                          (Hooke's Law / Spring)
V = IR                           (Ohm's Law)
PV = nRT                         (Ideal Gas Law)
ΔS ≥ 0                           (Second Law of Thermo)
F = GmM/r²                       (Gravitational Force)
E = hf                           (Photon Energy)
Δv = Isp × g₀ × ln(m₀/mf)       (Tsiolkovsky Rocket Equation)
```
