## Phase 0: Financial Literacy (Week 1-2)

### Key Concepts Everyone MUST Know
```
Income - Expenses = Savings
Savings × Investment Returns = Wealth
Wealth × Time = Financial Freedom

The #1 rule: SPEND LESS THAN YOU EARN
The #2 rule: INVEST THE DIFFERENCE
The #3 rule: START EARLY (compound interest)
```

### Compound Interest — The 8th Wonder
```
Future Value = P × (1 + r)^n

₹10,000/month invested at 12% annual return:
  After 10 years: ₹23.5 Lakhs
  After 20 years: ₹1.0 Crore
  After 30 years: ₹3.5 Crore

Starting 10 years earlier = 3.5x more money
```

### The 50-30-20 Rule
| Category | % of Income | Examples |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| **Needs** | 50% | Rent, food, transport, bills |
| **Wants** | 30% | Entertainment, dining, gadgets |
| **Invest** | 20% | Stocks, MFs, PPF, FD |

**Elon Musk version: 70% invest, 20% needs, 10% wants** 😤

## Phase 2: Stock Market (Week 7-14)

### How the Stock Market Works
```
Company needs money → Issues shares (IPO)
Shares trade on exchange (NSE/BSE)
Share price = what buyers will pay
You buy low, sell high (or hold forever)

Market Cap = Share Price × Total Shares
P/E Ratio = Share Price / Earnings Per Share
```

### Types of Analysis
| Type | What | Tools |
|------|------|-------|
| **Fundamental** | Company financials, moat, management | Annual reports, Screener.in |
| **Technical** | Charts, patterns, indicators | TradingView, RSI, MACD |
| **Quantitative** | Math models, algorithms | Python, statistics |

### Key Metrics to Evaluate a Stock
| Metric | Good Range | What It Means |
|--------|-----------|---------------|
| **P/E Ratio** | 10-25 | Price relative to earnings |
| **ROE** | >15% | How efficiently equity is used |
| **Debt/Equity** | <1 | Low debt is safer |
| **Revenue Growth** | >15% YoY | Company is growing |
| **Profit Margin** | >10% | Good profitability |
| **Promoter Holding** | >50% | Founders have skin in game |

### Investment Vehicles (India)
| Vehicle | Risk | Returns | Best For |
|---------|------|---------|----------|
| **FD** | Very Low | 6-7% | Emergency fund |
| **PPF** | Zero | 7.1% | Tax-free long-term |

| **ELSS MF** | Medium | 12-16% | Tax saving + returns |
| **Direct Stocks** | High | 15-25%+ | Active investing |
| **Crypto** | Very High | -50% to +500% | Speculation only |

## Phase 3: Advanced Investing (Week 15-24)

### Portfolio Construction
```
Conservative (Low Risk):
  60% Debt (FD, PPF, Debt MF)
  30% Equity (Index Funds)
  10% Gold

Balanced:
  40% Equity (Index + ELSS)
  30% Debt
  20% Direct Stocks
  10% Gold/International

Aggressive (Elon Mode):
  60% Direct Stocks
  20% Index Funds
  10% Startup Equity
  10% Crypto/Alternative
```

### Warren Buffett's Rules
1. **Rule 1**: Never lose money
2. **Rule 2**: Never forget Rule 1
3. **Buy wonderful companies at fair prices**
4. **Hold forever** (his favorite holding period)
5. **Be fearful when others are greedy, greedy when others are fearful**

### VC & Startup Investing (For Founders)
```
Valuation = How much your company is worth
Pre-money valuation: Value BEFORE investment
Post-money valuation: Value AFTER investment

Example:
  Pre-money: ₹5 Cr
  Investment: ₹1 Cr
  Post-money: ₹6 Cr
  Investor gets: 1/6 = 16.67% equity
  
Dilution: Your % decreases with each funding round
ESOP Pool: 10-15% reserved for employees
```
