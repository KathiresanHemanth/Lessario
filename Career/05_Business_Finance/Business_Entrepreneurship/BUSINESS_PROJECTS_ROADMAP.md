# Business & Entrepreneurship - Project-Based Roadmap

## Level 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-4)
### Project 1: Personal Budget & Cash Flow Tracker — ⭐
- Track income, expenses, savings rate. Understand cash flow.
### Project 2: Business Plan (1-Page Lean Canvas) — ⭐
- Pick any problem. Write: Problem, Solution, Metrics, Unfair Advantage.
### Project 3: Market Research Report — ⭐⭐
- Research a ₹1000 Cr market. TAM/SAM/SOM, competitors, SWOT.
### Project 4: Customer Discovery (Talk to 20 People) — ⭐⭐
- Validate a problem. Write findings report.

## Level 2: Build & Sell (Weeks 5-10)
### Project 5: Launch a Micro-SaaS (or Side Project) — ⭐⭐⭐
- Build a small tool, get 5 users. Free or paid.
### Project 6: Freelance & Earn First ₹10K Online — ⭐⭐⭐
- Fiverr, Upwork, Toptal. Prove you can earn independently.
### Project 7: Landing Page + Email List (100 signups) — ⭐⭐⭐
- Marketing funnel. Copywriting. Conversion.
### Project 8: Financial Model in Excel/Sheets — ⭐⭐⭐
- Project revenue, costs, profit for 3 years. Investor-ready.

## Level 3: Startup Operations (Weeks 11-18)
### Project 9: Incorporate a Company (Pvt Ltd) — ⭐⭐⭐
- Actually register on MCA, get GST, open bank account.
### Project 10: Pitch Deck (10 slides, investor-ready) — ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- Problem, Solution, Market, Traction, Team, Ask.
### Project 11: Get First 10 Paying Customers — ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- Revenue > 0 is the most important milestone.
### Project 12: Apply to Y Combinator / Startup Incubator — ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- Write application. Even if rejected, the process teaches you everything.

## Level 4: Scale (Weeks 19+)
### Project 13: Hire First Team Member — ⭐⭐⭐⭐
### Project 14: Setup Analytics & KPI Dashboard — ⭐⭐⭐⭐
### Project 15: Raise Angel/Pre-Seed Round — ⭐⭐⭐⭐
### Project 16: Achieve ₹1L Monthly Recurring Revenue — ⭐⭐⭐⭐

## Path
**Week 1-4**: Projects 1-4 → **Week 5-10**: Projects 5-8 → **Week 11-18**: Projects 9-12 → **Week 19+**: Projects 13-16
