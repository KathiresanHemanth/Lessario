> [!TIP]
> **Outreach Best Practices:**
> 1. **Email first** with a clear subject line: "Sports Industry Event – Partnership Opportunity"  
> 2. **Follow up by phone** within 3–5 business days  
> 3. **LinkedIn** for C-suite executives who don't have public emails  
> 4. **Attach a one-pager** event proposal deck with sponsorship tiers  
> 5. Use **partnerships@** or **info@** emails for initial cold outreach  
> 6. For **listed companies** — try investor relations email for formal corporate tie-ups
