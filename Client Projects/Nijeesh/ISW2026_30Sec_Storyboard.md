# India Sports Week 2026 - 30 Second Video Storyboard & Prompts

This storyboard breaks down the 30-second script into **8 dynamic, fast-paced shots** (approx. 3-4 seconds each). 

* Per your request, the **On-Screen Text** has been directly integrated into the **Nanobanana Pro (Image)** prompts so the text generates within the starting frame.
