Epi 1

**Scene 1: Neon Forest**

* **Meenukutti**: "Eh... aathula thanni varadhu ninnuduchu\!"  
* **Muttagose**: "Ayyoo\! thanni off aayiduchaa?"  
* **Suttapazham**: "Thanni vaasam yengayo dhoorathula iruku... aana thannidhaan illa."  
* **Kongini**: "Thanni surakkura edathula la yedho prachana irukku nu neaikkiren."  
* **Thanthiraa**: "Oruvela aaru vaththipoirukkumo?"  
* **Sssarapaambu**: "Ayyooo Ippo enna panrathu... Yaaru namma kaatta kaapaathuvaanga"  
* **Singu**: "My dear mirugangale, paravaigale, poochigale... Intha pazhaiyaaru thaan dhaan namma kaattoda uyirnaadi... Adhu illanaa namma yaarume illa... Neenga yaarum kavalai padatheenga... Oru rajavaa intha prachanaiya theerkka vendiyathu en kadamai"  
* **Singu**: "Muttagose, Thanthiraa, Kongini, Suttapazham, Meenukutti, Sssaarapaambu yellarum vaanga namakku velai vanthuruchu."

**Scene 2: Various Places in the Forest**

* **Singu**: "Meenukutti... Athu yenna satham nu paaru..."  
* **Meenukutti**: "Namma vazhiya marachu anga neriya marangal sarinju kedakku..."  
* **Mutaagose**: "Ipo naama epdi intha thadaiya kadakkurathu?"  
* **Kongini**: "Hey... Aathukulla laam kuppaiya kottakoodathu..."

**Scene 3: River Upstream (Flashback)**

* **Thanthiraa**: "Ayyayo... Odudaa Thanthiraa..."

**Scene 4: Swamp**

* **Singu**: "Yellarum paathu porumiayaa intha sadhuppu nilaththa kadanthu vaanga... Konjam thavarinaalum ithu nammala ulla izhtuthukkum..."  
* **Muttagose**: "Yaaraavthu enna kaapaathunga\! Intha saeththul enna gum pottu ottuna maadhiri irukku\!"  
* **Singu**: "Muttagose... Sssaarapambu vaala nalla irukkama pudichukko..."

**Scene 5: Mountains/Upstream**

* **Kongini**: "Nilacharivu aagi paaraigal nagarnthurukku"  
* **Muttagose**: "Idhu odaikalana aathula thanni thirumba varadhu."  
* **Thanthiraa**: "Intha pakkam thallunga apothaan nagarum"  
* **Kongini**: "Illa... Intha pakkam thallumpothuthaan thanni paayura thisaila kallum easy ah nagarum..."  
* **Singu**: "Kumi... Tisa... Nane... Saba... Sita... Tano... Nne... Tatu... Mbili... Moja..."  
* **Singu**: "IDEA\!"  
* **Singu**: "Paaraigala nammalaala thalla mudiyadhu. Aana thannioda thisaiya maatha mudiyum."  
* **Singu**: "Oru vaaikkaala uruvaakkalaam"

**Scene 7 & 8: Pazhaiyaaru River Forest (Return)**

* **Muttagose**: "Namma raja illa na intha kaadu thanni illadha kaadaa poirukum\!"  
* **Singu**: "Team work dhaan namma balam. Yellarum sernthu senjathunaalathaan ithu saathiyamaachu"  
* **Thanthiraa**: "Cha... Intha thadava thappichuttanga... Aduthamurai innum gavanamaa irukkanum..."  
* **Muttagose**: "Ayyoo... kuppai\!"
