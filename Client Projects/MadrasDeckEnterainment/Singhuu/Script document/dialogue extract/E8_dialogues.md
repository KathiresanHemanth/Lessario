# Dialogues from E8

* **Muttagose**: “Rendu perum sernthu Thiruppachi Aruvaala paattukku reels poda poraangala”
* **Suttapazham**: “Raja anga oru periya sanda thodanga poguthu pola…”
* **Thanthiraa**: “Ivanoda ellai manangala avan edathula pottutaa, intha edaththukaaga rendu perum sandai pottupaanunga… Singu intha prachanaiya nee yepdi samaalikkapora nu paapom…”
* **Singu**: “Niruthunga!”
* **Muttagose**: “Aathaadi… Vitta iniku lunch-ku muttagose poriyal saaptruvaan polayae”
* **Kongini**: “Raja… ivanga rendu perum ithu yaaroda ellai nu kuzhappaththula irukaanga”
* **Singu**: “My dear mirugangale, paravaigale, poochigale… Idhu oru saadharana prachanaiyilla… Itha ipdiaye vitta idhu oru por ah maara vaaippirukku”
* **Singu**: “Indha por nadandha… kaadu rendaagidum”
* **Singu**: “Meenukutti rendu koottaththaiyum ah yum track pannu​ Thanthiraa unnoda moppa sakthiya payan paduththi ivangaloda unmaiyaana idam yethu nu kandupidi.​ Konginiyum naanum intha onaaikalaiyum, kazhuththaipuligalyum pin thodaranthu porom”
* **Muttagose**: “Kandupudichuten”
* **Kongini**: “Avangalukku ippodhaikku oru ellai thevai Raja… Adhai ippo rendu perukkum podhuvaana oruthar kodukkanum…”
* **Muttagose**: “Avalodhaanaa… Ipo paarunga…”
* **Muttagose**: “Idhu podhumaa akka ellaiya mudivu panna…”
* **Kongini**: “Nari, Onaai, Kazhudhaippuli maathiri Kaatu Naai vagai mirugangal pala vidhaththula ellai kurichchuppaanga… Udal kazhivu, Oolai saththam, Nagaththaala keerappatta kodugal… Ithula yethu irunthaalum avangalukku avanga ellai edhu nu theriyum…”
* **Kongini**: “Raja… neenga oru perum por ah niruthitteenga…”
* **Singu**: “Por nadanthu adhula oru pakkaththukku mattum kidaikkirathu vetri illa Kongini… Porae nadakkama irukkaradhu dhaan unmaiyaana vetri.”
* **Thanthiraa**: “Idhuvum fail… Thirumbavum varuven Singu… Pudhu prachanaiyoda…”
* **Muttagose**: “Ennoda ellaikku ulla varaadhinga!”
