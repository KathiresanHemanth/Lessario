# Meenukutti - Dialogues (Epi 5 - 8)

## Epi 5

* **Meenukutti**: “Wind direction romba strong ah irukku!”
* **Meenukutti**: “Raja… yaarum illa… aana signals varudhu!”
* **Meenukutti**: “Raja… signals yethuvum correct ah illa”
* **Meenukutti**: “Sariyaana uyarathula signal kudukkanum”

## Epi 6

* **Meenukutti**: “Raja… yaarum thaakkala… Kovamaa illa… aana ellarum pei bayathula mattum irukkura maadhiri irukku!”

## Epi 7

* **Meenukutti**: “Raja… anga yaaro asaiyura maathiri irunthuchu”
