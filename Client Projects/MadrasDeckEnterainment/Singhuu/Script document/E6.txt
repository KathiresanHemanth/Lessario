Singu - The Raja
Episode 6 - A Panic Wave
Runtime: ~22 minutes
Written by - Santhosh Mathevan

SCENE 1 – INT/EXT - NIGHT - THANHTHIRAA’S CAVE & OTHER PLACES
Thanthiraa is seriously working on something in his cave.
He’s collecting a few objects from his shelf, table, and floor.
He walks into the forest.
●​ throwing objects from the hiding on to animal groups.
●​ creating shadows among monkeys.
●​ scaring them with horror moments.
The forest panics.
Thanthira:​
“Ipo enna panna pora Singu…”
And laughs villainously.
The forest starts to get more and more wild.
SCENE 2 – EXT – DAY – ROCK BASIN WATER HOLE
A natural rock basin filled with water. Surrounded by uneven stones.
Animals panic everywhere.
Muttagose is seeing water animals panicking.​
Suttapazham is trying to calm down a few monkeys.​
Meenukutti gliding low across water to calm the deers.
Singu is holding Sssaaarapaambu on his shoulders and by his side is
Kongini, and they’re observing everything calmly.
At that time, under the water… a large shape slowly shifts.

Muttagose notices that and hops towards Singu.
MUTTAGOSE​
“Raja… Raja… Thannikkulla naalu kaal vecha thimingalam onnu varudhu…”
KONGINI​
“Thimingalaththukku kaal laam kidaiyaathu Muttagose”
MUTTAGOSE​
“Appo adhu yaaru akka?”
The water ripples and slowly moves forward.
A hippopotamus (Sigmundi) rises above the water level.
Sigmundi is half-submerged. Only her eyes are visible.
No one else notices.
She is the only calm animal in the whole chaos.
A flock of birds bursts out screaming.
Deer run across.
Monkeys jump from tree to tree, shouting.
Within seconds… chaos spreads even wilder.
Muttagose:​
“Avanga mattum yepdi amaidhiyaa padhattamaagama irukaanga?”
Animals start running in all directions.
Sigmundi slowly lifts her head out of the water and looks around.
Sigmundi (softly):​
“Padhattamaagi odunaa prachana theeraadhu…”
No one listens.
Panic builds even more with every second.

SCENE 3 – EXT – DAY – WATER HOLE / CHAOS ZONE
Animals colliding.

Dust rising.
Small animals getting pushed.
Muttagose tries to stop a group.
Gets dragged.
Muttagose:​
“Ayyoooo! Brake pudikkatha thanni lorry maathiri odraanga!”
Suttapazham stands in front of a deer herd.
Gets knocked aside.
Suttapazham:​
“Enna da idhu! Deepavali highway traffic ah?!”
Meenukutti flies up. Looks around.
Animals from a distant place shout “Kaattukkulla pei vanthuruchu…”,
“Pei adichuruchu…”
Meenukutti returns confused.
Meenukutti:​
“Raja… yaarum thaakkala… Kovamaa illa… aana ellarum pei bayathula
mattum irukkura maadhiri irukku!”
Muttagose​
“Yetho artham puriyaatha pudhu padam paattu keturupaangalo?”
Kongini:​
“Idhu logic illama nadakkudhu…”
Muttagose​
“Apdina ithu kandippa yetho pei padathoda anjaavathu aaraavathu part
paathu athuku vantha reaction ah thaan irukum…”
Sigmundi(calmly):​
“Idhu peiyum illa pisaasum illa… Yerkanavae nadantha oru
prachanaiyoda reaction…”
Sigmundi looks at Sigmundi.
She’s calm. Watching the animals.
Singu climbs higher onto a rock. Roars.

Nearby animals stand still.
Singu:​
“My dear mirugangale, paravaigale, poochigale… Namma kaattoda
amaidhaan namma ellarukkumaana sudhandhiram… Atha kaappaatha vendiya
neram vandhuruchu…”
Animals listening.
Singu:​
“Meenukutti! Evalo dhoorathukku intha prachanai irukku nu paaru!”​
“Kongini! Movement pattern note pannu!”​
“Sssaarapaambu! Nila adhirvu yepdi irukku sollu!”​
“Thanthiraa nee mirugangal pora thisaiya track pannu”
Sigmundi walks slowly beside Singu.
No instruction given to her.
SCENE 4 – EXT – DAY – RUNNING STREAM CHANNEL
A narrow rocky water path.
Animals running through water blindly.
Water splashing everywhere.
Thanthiraa jumps in front.
Gets swept.
Thanthiraa​
“Ayyoo!!”
Singu holds him right away.
Muttagose blocks the path.
Gets pushed aside.
Suttapazham:​
“Ellu vaya pookkalayae… Muttagose-nu kooda paakkalayae…”
Muttagose gets up and looks angry at Suttapazham.
Sigmundi steps into water.
Stands still.

Some animals slow… but not enough.
Singu observes how Sigmundi moves and gets an idea.
Singu:​
“Yellarum ennoda vegaththukku yetha maathiri odunga…”
Muttagose:​
“Unga vegaththukku odanumna intha kaattulayae adhu orutharaala thaan
mudiyum…”
Muttagose says that with a prideful face.
Then Singu suddenly runs ahead of the herd.
Muttagose looks at Singu’s speed.
Muttagose​
“Aana adhu sathyama naan illa”
Singu starts running fast initially, and then slightly slower, then
increases speed a little.
Step by step.
The herd starts matching his pace according to how fast or slow he
runs.
Singu slowly turns sideways.
Flow direction changes.
Stampede weakens.
Animals slow down.
Sigmundi watches quietly.
SCENE 5 – EXT – DAY – FEAR LOOP ROCK ZONE
A circular rocky patch.
Animals running in loops.
Dust cloud.
Muttagose tries shouting.

Gets pulled into loop.
Muttagose:​
“Enna da idhu mega moottamaa… Illana vazhi maari Delhi ku yethum
vanthutomaa”
The animals couldn’t be controlled.
Kongini:​
“Stop pannunga! Stop pannunga!”
No one listens.
Meenukutti flies above.
Signals.
No use.
Sigmundi (softly):​
“Yaarum pinnadi vara maataanga…”
Animals continue running.
Singu kneels.
Observes footprints.
Singu realises.
He jumps into center.
Runs in the opposite direction.
Collides lightly with one animal.
Breaks pattern.
Blocks one exit.
Loop collapses.
Animals stop.
Muttagose dizzy. Imaginary carrots run around his head.
Muttagose:​
“Naan ippo enga irukken…?”

SCENE 6 – EXT – DAY – CLIFF EDGE
High tension.
A herd running straight toward a cliff.
Dust everywhere.
Muttagose:​
“Ayyooo! Pallaththakku!”
Suttapazham freezes.
Kongini panics.
Meenukutti dives to warn.
No use.
Sigmundi walks near the edge.
Stands calmly.
Herd is still charging.
Singu closes his eyes and meditates.
Everything slows.
Singu:​
Kumi…
Tisa…
Nane…
Saba…
Sita…
Tano…
Nne…
Tatu…
Mbili…
Moja…

He opens eyes.
He’s Super Singu now.
Singu runs.
Straight toward the herd.
Jumps onto a rock.
Roars.
Then leaps diagonally into their path.
The herd shifts slightly.
Not enough.
He takes a banyan tree root and locks all directions..
Guiding movement.
Herd splits.
Stops before cliff.
Dust settles.
Singu’s shadow silhouette shows in dust.
Animals fear the shadow and call it a ghost.
Singu:​
“Nizhal-ah paathu neenga pei nu bayapduringa nanbaragale… Intha
ulagam uyir ullavargalukkaanadhu mattum thaan… Pei apdinu onnum
kidaiyaathu… Namma manasu athukku puriyaatha vishayaththa paathu
bayapadum… Naama porumaiyaa irunthaa ipo nadantha maathiri, namma
manasukku yellaam thaanaa puriyum… Adhuku naama padhattaththa kammi
pannanum…”
Muttagose:​
“Adhaana. Sonna yenga kekuraanga… Chinnapullathanama pei pisasu nu
nambikkitu… Athellam onnum kidaiyaathu… Yellarum enna maathiri
dhairiyama irunga”
Sigmundi looks at Muttagose. Muttagose smiles like he’s brave.
Animals calm down.

SCENE 7 – EXT – DAY – CALM FOREST
Silence. Animals breathing heavily. Slowly calming.
Muttagose looks at Sigmundi.
Muttagose:​
“Neenga yaaru akka…?”
Sigmundi smiles.
Sigmundi:​
“Naan oru therapist…”
Pause.
Muttagose looks confused.
Sigmundi:​
“Odambukku oru prachanaina doctor help pannuvaangala… Adhu maathiri
naan manasukku prachanai vanthaa help pannuven.”
Singu:​
“Muttagose… Bayam prachanai illa… Atha naamaa epdi kaatromngrathu
dhaan mukkiyam.”
Sigmundi nods.
Thanthiraa is disappointed.
FINAL GAG SCENE – EXT - DAY – WATER HOLE
Muttagose sees his reflection.
Jumps back.
Muttagose:​
“Ayyoo!”
Looks again.
Muttagose:​
“Ada naan dhaana adhu…”
Everyone laughs.
FADE OUT.
