Singu - The Raja
Episode 8 - A Territory War
Runtime: ~22 minutes
Written by - Santhosh Mathevan

SCENE 1 – EXT – DAY – RED SOIL CLEARING
A dry reddish terrain area (new location).
Animals are casually active.
Muttagose is digging holes and gets into the hole, but the wind blows
and covers the hole. He comes out in panic and takes a huge breath.
Suttapazham is resting in the shade and laughing at Muttagose.
Meenukutti is flying low.
Singu watching.
Suddenly…
A loud roar-like call echoes.
Followed by another sound from opposite direction.
Tension rises.
Two groups appear at distance:
Wolf pack and a Hyena pack.
Both entering the same clearing.
Muttagose:​
“Rendu perum sernthu Thiruppachi Aruvaala paattukku reels poda
poraangala”
The gang looks keenly at the confrontation.
Both packs look aggressive.

Suttapazham:​
“Raja anga oru periya sanda thodanga poguthu pola…”
Animals slowly back away.
Thanthiraa smiles in this chaos.
SCENE 2 – EXT – NIGHT – MUSHROOM GARDEN
FLASHBACK
Thanthiraa the previous night:
●​ Moves scent markers
●​ Dragged food remains
●​ Created false territory signs
Thanthiraa:​
“Ivanoda ellai manangala avan edathula pottutaa, intha edaththukaaga
rendu perum sandai pottupaanunga… Singu intha prachanaiya nee yepdi
samaalikkapora nu paapom…”
Back to present.
SCENE 3 – EXT - DAY - MUSHROOM GARDEN
An animal stand-off is underway
Wolves stomp ground.
Hyenas circle.
Growls. Snorts.
Dust rises.
Singu steps forward.
Singu:​
“Niruthunga!”
No one listens.
Muttagose goes between the two packs and tries to stop them with a
hand signal.

A hyena looks at Muttagose as a delicious meal and drools.
Muttagose:​
“Aathaadi… Vitta iniku lunch-ku muttagose poriyal saaptruvaan
polayae”
And Muttagose runs away from that place and hides behind Singu.
Kongini:​
“Raja… ivanga rendu perum ithu yaaroda ellai nu kuzhappaththula
irukaanga”
Singu realises
This is not random​
This is territorial war trigger
Singu climbs a rock.
Singu:​
“My dear mirugangale, paravaigale, poochigale… Idhu oru saadharana
prachanaiyilla… Itha ipdiaye vitta idhu oru por ah maara
vaaippirukku”
Pause.
Singu:​
“Indha por nadandha… kaadu rendaagidum”
He assigns:
Singu:​
“Meenukutti rendu koottaththaiyum ah yum track pannu​
Thanthiraa unnoda moppa sakthiya payan paduththi ivangaloda
unmaiyaana idam yethu nu kandupidi.​
Konginiyum naanum intha onaaikalaiyum, kazhuththaipuligalyum pin
thodaranthu porom”
SCENE 4 – EXT - DAY - MAGARANTHA VANAM
Area filled with mixed scent trails.
Wolves follow one trail.
Hyenas follow another.
But finally both are leading into the same zone.

Kongini tries to identify the original path.
Too many overlaps.
Thanthiraa follows scent.
He himself gets confused.
Muttagose randomly follows a scent.
Everyone looks at Muttagose seriously.
For a moment, everyone thinks Muttagose has found the right spot.
Finally, Muttagose enters a bush and searches for something
seriously.
Muttagose:​
“Kandupudichuten”
Comes out with a carrot.
Everyone looks at him in anger and disappointment.
Singu observes ground disturbance.
Looks at the print:
The real path has deeper hoof marks and the fake path is
surface-level drag
He separates true vs fake trails.
SCENE 5 – EXT - DAY - MADU MUNAI
Both groups moving toward a narrow rock pass.
If they enter at same time there will be stampedes.
Muttagose tries blocking.
He’s thrown out into the sky, then falls back, then shot back into
the sky, and then again hits the ground and gets caught by Kongini.
Suttapazham stands in middle.
Gets pushed.

Meenukutti tries distraction.
No use.
Animals panic.
Singu sits.
Amid chaos.
Kumi…
Tisa…
Nane…
Saba…
Sita…
Tano…
Nne…
Tatu…
Mbili…
Moja…
He opens eyes.
He’s Super Singu.
Singu runs ahead.
Creates dust cloud by spinning on loose soil.
Visibility drops.
Muttagose sneezes, and the dust on his body spreads away in that
vibration and forms the shape of his body.
Thanthiraa giggles at that.
Muttagose gets angry.
Groups slow down.

They almost stop without able to see the path ahead.
Then Singu redirects one group sideways.
SCENE 6 – EXT - DAY - KATTAANTHARAI PAGUDHI
Open ground.
Both groups are now face-to-face.
High tension.
No stopping now.
Team fails again:
●​ shouting ignored
●​ movement ignored
●​ noise ignored
Kongini:​
“Avangalukku ippodhaikku oru ellai thevai Raja… Adhai ippo rendu
perukkum podhuvaana oruthar kodukkanum…”
Muttagose:​
“Avalodhaanaa… Ipo paarunga…”
Muttagose picks up a few twigs and goes in between to form a line
with the twigs arranged on the ground.
The animals run over Muttagose, and the twigs collapse.
Singu realises:
They are not angry at each other but, they are defending territory
So—
He removes the conflict itself
Singu moves between them.
Roars.
Marks a new neutral boundary zone
Then:

●​ scratches ground visibly
●​ creates clear dividing line
●​ guides both groups away from center
Then signals:
Two different safe territories.
Muttagose:​
“Idhu podhumaa akka ellaiya mudivu panna…”
Kongini:​
“Nari, Onaai, Kazhudhaippuli maathiri Kaatu Naai vagai mirugangal
pala vidhaththula ellai kurichchuppaanga… Udal kazhivu, Oolai
saththam, Nagaththaala keerappatta kodugal… Ithula yethu irunthaalum
avangalukku avanga ellai edhu nu theriyum…”
Muttagose looks at the paw mark of Singu on the ground.
The groups slowly retreat.
War avoided.
SCENE 7 – EXT - DAY - RED SOIL CLEARING
Forest calms.
Animals relieved.
Kongini:​
“Raja… neenga oru perum por ah niruthitteenga…”
Singu:​
“Por nadanthu adhula oru pakkaththukku mattum kidaikkirathu vetri
illa Kongini… Porae nadakkama irukkaradhu dhaan unmaiyaana vetri.”
SCENE 8 – EXT - DAY - A BUSH IN A DISTANCE
Thanthiraa watches.
Impressed, but slightly frustrated.
Thanthiraa:​
“Idhuvum fail… Thirumbavum varuven Singu… Pudhu prachanaiyoda…”

FINAL GAG SCENE – EXT - DAY - RED SOIL CLEARING
Muttagose draws his own “territory line”
Stands proudly.
A few ants go by that in a line and step over it casually.
Muttagose:​
“Ennoda ellaikku ulla varaadhinga!”
The ants bite Muttagose's leg, and he hops away in pain.
Everyone laughs.
