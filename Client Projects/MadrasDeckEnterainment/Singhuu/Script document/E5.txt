Singu - The Raja
Episode 5 - The Broken Signal
Runtime: ~22 minutes
Written by - Santhosh Mathevan

SCENE 1 – EXT – DAY – WIND RIDGE
A high rocky ridge with strong wind blowing continuously.
Muttagose is flying a dry leaf like a kite.
Suttapazham is struggling to stand straight.
Meenukutti glides smoothly through wind currents.
Singu is watching everything calmly.
Suddenly Meenukutti feels a tension…
MEENUKUTTI​
“Wind direction romba strong ah irukku!”
Sssaarapaambu sensing ground calmly.
Kongini is sketching how wind moves through rocks.
Singu is observing everything.
One of the wind tunnels produces a sharp warning-like sound.
Almost like a danger signal.
Everyone freezes.
MUTTAGOSE​
“Yaaro warning kudukraanga?”
Another wind tunnel produces a different warning sound
From another direction.
Then another.

Muttagose’s head blows up for every warning sound.
Finally, it becomes very hugged and he starts to fly and float in the
air like a balloon.
Kongini pounces and grabs his leg and pulls him down.
Now multiple “signals” echo across the ridge.
All different.
All confusing.
Meenukutti flies up to check.
No predator.
No danger.
MEENUKUTTI​
“Raja… yaarum illa… aana signals varudhu!”
Singu looks around.
Listens carefully.
Wind intensifies.
Sounds overlap.
Animals in distance start reacting to these sounds.
Some begin running.
Some hiding.
Confusion spreads.
SINGU (softly)​
“Idhu iyarkkaiyaa vara saththam illa…”
MEENUKUTTI​
“Raja… signals yethuvum correct ah illa”
Singu looks around.
Confusion spreads.

SCENE 2 – EXT – DAY – RIDGE
Multiple wrong signals across forest.
●​ false warning calls
●​ wrong directions
●​ panic movements
SSSAARAPAAMBU​
“Ground vibration kooda mismatch aagudhu…”
MUTTAGOSE​
“Apo antha vibration ah use panni Arabic Kuththukku dance aadalaam
vaanga…”
And laughs at his own joke.
KONGINI​
“Yaaro naama signals ah maathirukanga…”
Singu observes.
Singu jumps onto a rock.
SINGU​
“My dear mirugangale, paravaigale, poochigale… Namakku naduvula
irukku signals baadhikkapaduthu… Itha ippadiye vitta nama
koottangalukku naduvula pala vagaiyaana prachanai varum… Udaney itha
sari seiyyanum…”
The gang listens.
SINGU​
“Meenukutti! Edhu la irunthu intha prachana start aagudhu nu paaru!”​
“Kongini! Pattern identify pannu!”​
“Thanthiraa! Ground truth sollu!”
Action begins.
SCENE 3 – EXT – DAY – FALSE STAMPEDE CORRIDOR
A downhill rocky path.
Nilgiri Thar getting stuck in the valley bottom without guidance.
No reason.

Muttagose jumps in front to guide them.
MUTTAGOSE​
“Ayyoo! Inga oru periya manushan vanthu guide panren… Yaarum madhikka
maatenguraanga…”
SINGU​
“Avangalukku ipo sariyaana signal kudukkanum…”
Singu runs towards a bamboo tree, breaks its stem, and makes a few
holes in it.
He blows the bamboo stem, and a sound signal comes out of it.
Animals are about to crash into a rock wall.
Muttagose tries to stop an antelope and gets crushed onto a rock and
is completely flattened.
Suttapazham and Kongini block all the other paths except an escape
route.
Singu runs ahead.
Climbs a side rock.
Jumps into the path.
Runs diagonally.
The Thar flock shifts direction.
The flock is rescued.
SCENE 4 – EXT – DAY – SIGNAL CONFUSION ZONE
Rock formations are blocking the sunlight signal to giraffes.
Meenukutti tries to give a signal from a height.
But the giraffes couldn’t raise their neck above that limit as the
whole area is covered with dense forest trees.
MEENUKUTTI​
“Sariyaana uyarathula signal kudukkanum”
Kongini tries guiding the giraffes.

Animals move in the opposite direction.
KONGINI​
“Thappaana thisaila poreenga! Nillunga!”
Muttagose runs in circles.
MUTTAGOSE​
“Ayyoo! Loop la maattikkichu… Oduraanga, Idikkiraanga, Nikkiraanga,
Repeat-uh…”
Chaos everywhere.
Singu climbs to the top of a tree.
Singu poses and closes eyes.
Everything slows.
Kumi…
Tisa…
Nane…
Saba…
Sita…
Tano…
Nne…
Tatu…
Mbili…
Moja…
He’s Super Singu now.
From the top of the tree, he roars aloud.
The vibration of his sound reverberates over the trees and the dense
trees move to make way for sunlight.
Giraffes find a way to get out.

They move on to the next obstacle.
SCENE 5 – EXT – DAY – FOREST FLOOR
Sssaarapaambu finds disturbed ground markers.
Kongini studies patterns.
Meenukutti tracks paths.
KONGINI​
“Signals ellam yaaro damage pannirukanga… Yethuvume seeraana muraila
illa”.
Thanthiraa smiles in silence.

FLASHBACK

THANTHIRAA:​

Back to present.
SCENE 7 – EXT – DAY – FINAL DROP ZONE
A large group of baby boars is running toward a rocky drop.
High speed.
Dust rising again.
SUTTAPAZHAM​
“Odaatheenga!”
No response.
Muttagose tries to block a baby boar.

But he gets stuck between two horns of a baby boar and sits on his
nose.
That baby boar is about to clash with another boar.
Now Muttagose holds the two horns like car steering and tries driving
the boar away.
The boar herd gets closer to the edge.
Singu runs ahead.
Climbs a rock.
Roars.
Then—
He creates a clear directional signal using a horn left by Thanthiraa
earlier in that spot:
The baby boars follow his
●​ body movement
●​ sound
●​ position
They focus on him.
They follow the correct path.
The entire group turns.
Stops before falling.
SCENE 8 – EXT – DAY – CALM FOREST
Silence returns.
Animals are breathing heavily.
All the wind tunnels and signal systems are being restored.
All animals work for the that.
KONGINI​
“Raja… neenga signal system ah sari panniteenga…”

SINGU​
“Namma yellarum sernthu thaan pannunom… Signal strong ah irundha…
kaadu strong ah irukkum.”
Thanthiraa is observing from a distance.
THANTHIRAA​
“Hmmm… Idhuvum fail…”
FINAL GAG SCENE – EXT - NIGHT – WIND RIDGE
The animals celebrate the calm forest.
Muttagose tries to give an “official signal.”
Animals run in the right direction now.
FADE OUT.
