# Character Breakdown and Episode Summaries

## Episode 2: Herb for the Herd
**Summary:** Singu and his friends navigate a treacherous mountain to find a healing mushroom to save a poisoned elephant herd.

* **Singu:** The Raja
* **Kongini:** Female Gorilla (Studies patterns/runs a tree-shade school)
* **Muttagose:** A hare/rabbit (implied later by the tortoise and hare race)
* **Thanthiraa:** Fox
* **Suttapazham:** Camel
* **Meenukutti:** A flying bird/animal
* **Sssaarapaambu:** A snake
* **Gajarani:** An elephant
* **Starlings:** A murmuring flock of birds
