## 🦁 Project Overview

The **Nama Thala Singhuu** series follows the adventures of **Singu**, the Raja of the forest, and a diverse council of animal friends. Together, they navigate crises, solve mysteries, and protect their forest home — promoting themes of leadership, cooperation, and environmental stewardship to young Tamil audiences.

The series is produced using **AI-assisted animation** pipelines, leveraging tools like Google AI Studio and Flow for storyboarding, character consistency, and visual generation.

## 🛠 Development Workflow

1. **Scripting** — Narratives are drafted and refined in `Main Files/Script document/`.
2. **Character Design** — 3D concept art and turnaround sheets managed in `Main Files/Animals Sheets/`.
3. **Storyboarding** — Visual sequences built using 3×3 grid systems optimized for AI visualization tools (NanoBananaPro / Google Flow).
4. **AI Animation** — Frames and sequences generated using AI-assisted pipelines for consistent character rendering.
5. **Post-Production** — Voice-over (Tamil), sound design, and final compositing.
6. **Game Integration** — Assets and narratives ported to the `SinghuuGame/` project for interactive adaptation.
