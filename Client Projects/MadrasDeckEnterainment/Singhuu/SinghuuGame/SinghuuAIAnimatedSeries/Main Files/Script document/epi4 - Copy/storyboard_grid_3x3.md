## Detailed Frame Breakdown for Production

### [Row 1: The Setup]

**1. Cell [1,1] - The Rock Plateau**
- **Shot Type:** Extreme Wide Shot (EWS)
- **Visual Description:** Sun-drenched, vibrant savannah plateau. Warm colors.
- **Action:** Muttagose and Thanthiraa are slow-racing in the foreground with umbrellas.

**2. Cell [1,2] - The Omen**
- **Shot Type:** Long Shot (LS)
- **Visual Description:** Heavy silhouette of Moothavar (tortoise) appearing at the rock edge. 
- **Mood:** Mysterious, weighted.

**3. Cell [1,3] - Converging Waves**
- **Shot Type:** Aerial POV / EWS
- **Visual Description:** Two massive, thundering dust clouds approaching from opposite ends of a green valley.

---

### [Row 2: The Struggle]

**4. Cell [2,1] - The Boulder Trap**
- **Shot Type:** Group Shot / High Angle
- **Visual Description:** Grey, jagged boulders. Harsh, high-contrast sunlight. 
- **Action:** The team looks small and vulnerable as the ground shifts beneath them.

**5. Cell [2,2] - The Anchor**
- **Shot Type:** Low Angle Hero Shot
- **Visual Description:** Singu looks powerful and immovable. 
- **Action:** Singu is firmly planted, catching Balarani/Kongini as they jump.

**6. Cell [2,3] - Sensory Decision**
- **Shot Type:** Extreme Close-Up (ECU) transition to Visual FX
- **Visual Description:** Singu’s eye closed. A blue/red vibration overlay appears on screen. 
- **Mood:** Focus, "Zen" moment.

---

### [Row 3: The Climax & Resolution]

**7. Cell [3,1] - The Roar**
- **Shot Type:** Low Angle / Action Shot
- **Visual Description:** Singu in "Super" mode at the peak. Golden glow. 
- **Action:** Mouth wide, visible sound-ripples (shockwaves) distorting the air.

**8. Cell [3,2] - Saving the Forest**
- **Shot Type:** High Angle / Top-Down
- **Visual Description:** Symmetrical split of the two herds. 
- **Action:** Rhinos to the left, Buffalo to the right. Dustin clouds billowing.

**9. Cell [3,3] -The Mischief Reveal**
- **Shot Type:** Medium Shot (Flashback)
- **Visual Description:** Oversaturated colors, blur edges. 
- **Action:** Thanthiraa smirking behind a bush, clicking his umbrella open.
