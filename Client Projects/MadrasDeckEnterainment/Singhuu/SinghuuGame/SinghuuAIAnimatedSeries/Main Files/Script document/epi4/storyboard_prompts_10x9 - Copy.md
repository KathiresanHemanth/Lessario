# Episode 4: 10-Prompt Storyboard (3x3 Grid Format)

This document provides **10 master prompts** to generate storyboard sheets. Each prompt is designed for a **3x3 grid** (9 shots per prompt), covering the entire 22-minute episode of *Singu - The Raja*.

---

### Prompt 1: The Rhythmic Plateau (0:00 – 2:30)
**Concept:** Peaceful daily life and training.
**Prompt:** A 3x3 storyboard grid, high-end 3D stylized cinematic animation style, expressive CGI character features. Tiles show: (1) WS Rock Plateau at dawn, (2) Muttagose and Thanthiraa slow-racing with umbrellas, (3) Close-up on Suttapazham balancing on one leg, (4) Meenukutti diving through wind currents, (5) Sssaarapaambu drawing patterns in sand with tail, (6) Kongini watching from a distance, (7) Singu watching with a regal smile, (8) Slow tracking shot of animals training, (9) Medium shot of everyone laughing together. Vibrant lighting and sun-drenched professional render.

### Prompt 2: The Arrival of the Omen (2:30 – 4:30)
**Concept:** The shift from play to tension.
**Prompt:** A 3x3 storyboard grid, 3D stylized high-end CGI animation. Tiles show: (1) Small pebbles on the ground jittering, (2) Sssaarapaambu snaps his head up alertly, (3) The group pauses and looks around, (4) A water puddle vibrating violently, (5) Singu looking toward the horizon with narrowing eyes, (6) Slow pan to a sharp rock edge, (7) Silhouette of Moothavar the old tortoise appearing, (8) Moothavar walking with heavy deliberate steps, (9) Close-up on Moothavar’s grave, weathered face. Moody cinematic lighting, high texture detail.

### Prompt 3: The Twin Waves (4:30 – 6:30)
**Concept:** Meenukutti’s aerial discovery.
**Prompt:** A 3x3 storyboard grid, premium 3D stylized animation. Tiles show: (1) Meenukutti taking off from the plateau, (2) Aerial POV looking down at the green canopy, (3) Two massive dust clouds on opposite horizons, (4) Meenukutti’s eyes widening in shock, (5) Extreme CU of charging buffalo herd, (6) Parallel cut to charging rhino herd, (7) The narrow valley between them (the death trap), (8) Meenukutti diving back toward the den, (9) Singu waiting at the entrance of the cave. Dynamic camera angles, wide-scale CGI environments.

### Prompt 4: The Strategy (6:30 – 8:30)
**Concept:** Leadership and role assignment.
**Prompt:** A 3x3 storyboard grid, expressive 3D stylized CGI character animation. Tiles show: (1) Singu standing central in the dim cave, (2) Meenukutti panting and reporting news, (3) Moothavar emerging from shadows, (4) Moothavar explaining "Survival of the Fittest", (5) Singu’s eyes closed, processing the weight, (6) Singu jumping onto the high rock ledge, (7) Singu pointing at the team members, (8) Balarani entering and reporting status, (9) The whole team standing together in unity. Professional sub-surface scattering and soft cave lighting.

### Prompt 5: The Stone Field (8:30 – 10:30)
**Concept:** Crossing the unstable terrain.
**Prompt:** A 3x3 storyboard grid, 3D stylized high-end cinematic render. Tiles show: (1) WS of the Moving Stone Field, (2) Singu’s paws stepping firmly on stones, (3) Muttagose looking down at precarious rocks (POV), (4) Muttagose’s foot slipping, (5) Suttapazham accidentally triggering a rockslide, (6) A cascade of stones rolling down the slope, (7) Thanthiraa wobbling and clutching his umbrella, (8) The team frozen on separate rock "islands", (9) Extreme CU on Singu’s calm eyes. Harsh desert lighting, high-quality particle effects for dust.

### Prompt 6: Singu the Anchor (10:30 – 12:30)
**Concept:** Physical strength and pathfinding.
**Prompt:** A 3x3 storyboard grid, premium 3D stylized animation style. Tiles show: (1) Visual effect highlight on "fixed" rocks in Singu’s vision, (2) Singu jumping with precision, (3) Singu planting feet on a massive immovable boulder, (4) Low angle shot of Singu as a physical anchor, (5) One by one, animals leaping toward him, (6) Singu catching Kongini mid-air, (7) Thanthiraa reaching safety, (8) The team assembled on firm ground, (9) Wide shot of the team looking back at the chaotic field. Heroic lighting and expressive character poses.

### Prompt 7: Reading the Earth (12:30 – 14:30)
**Concept:** Distinguishing the threats.
**Prompt:** A 3x3 storyboard grid, 3D stylized high-quality CGI. Tiles show: (1) The team at a massive rock fork, (2) Kongini trying to use logic on the terrain, (3) Muttagose sweating and panicking, (4) A massive cloud of dust erupting in the distance, (5) Singu pressing his front paw into the dirt, (6) Stylized "vibration map" overlay (Blue vs Red waves), (7) Singu’s ear twitching, isolating sound, (8) Singu’s eyes snapping open, (9) Singu pointing to the right path with authority. High texture detail on fur and terrain.

### Prompt 8: The Final Stretch (14:30 – 16:30)
**Concept:** Racing against the collision.
**Prompt:** A 3x3 storyboard grid, cinematic 3D stylized animation. Tiles show: (1) The team sprinting through a narrow pass, (2) Dutch angle shot of the canyon walls, (3) Light at the end of the tunnel, (4) The buffalo herd enters the screen from opposite side, (5) Singu leading the pack out of the tunnel, (6) The valley floor view from the edge, (7) The two herds seconds from impact, (8) The other animals waving and shouting to stop them, (9) Singu sitting amidst the chaos, closing eyes. Fast-paced motion blur and dramatic lighting.

### Prompt 9: The Great Collision Avoided (16:30 – 19:30)
**Concept:** Super Singu’s intervention.
**Prompt:** A 3x3 storyboard grid, high-end 3D stylized CGI, epic scale. Tiles show: (1) Bullet-time slow motion of the stampede, (2) Singu entering "Super" mode with golden glow, (3) Tactical overlay of the ridge lines, (4) Singu blurring past other animals at high speed, (5) Singu at the peak of the ridge, chest expanding, (6) The Great Roar with visible shockwaves, (7) Kongini charging the flank of the rhinos, (8) Herds moving like streams around the ridge, (9) Singu standing victorious as the dust settles. Magical golden VFX and lighting.

### Prompt 10: The Truth and the Race (19:30 – 22:00)
**Concept:** Resolution and the "Final Gag".
**Prompt:** A 3x3 storyboard grid, 3D stylized expressive character animation. Tiles show: (1) The animals gather back at Singu’s den, (2) Moothavar delivering the final lesson, (3) Flashback: Thanthiraa hiding behind a bush, (4) Thanthiraa popping his umbrella to spook the herds, (5) Thanthiraa smirking at the chaos he caused, (6) The "Finish Line" drawn in the sand on the plateau, (7) Muttagose and Moothavar lined up to race, (8) Muttagose tripping over his feet for a carrot, (9) Moothavar crossing the line slowly but victoriously. Warm golden hour lighting, comical clay-like squash and stretch features.
