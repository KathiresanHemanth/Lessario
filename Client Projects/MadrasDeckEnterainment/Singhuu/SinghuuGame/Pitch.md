# Pitch Deck: Nama Thala Singhuu — Forest Run
## *Our Leader's Dash*

---

## 1. Executive Summary
**Nama Thala Singhuu: Forest Run** is a high-octane 2.5D endless runner for Android and iOS, based on the AI-animated series **Nama Thala Singhuu** airing on **Sun Network's Chutti TV**. 

By combining addictive "run and dodge" mechanics with a unique **real-time character swapping system**, the game offers a premium branded experience for the 100 million+ Tamil-speaking market.

---

## 2. The Market Opportunity
- **Chutti TV Dominance:** Leading Tamil children's channel in Tamil Nadu.
- **Proven Model:** Indian IP-based runners (like Chhota Bheem) consistently outperform global hits locally.
- **Accessibility:** Optimized for budget-to-mid-range Android smartphones common in Tamil households.

---

## 3. The Product: "The Council System"
The USP of the game is the **Council System**. Players don't just pick one character; they take a **team**.

### Key Features:
- **Real-Time Swapping:** Tap mid-run to instantly swap between characters (Singu, Meenukutti, Thanthiraa).
- **Episode-Link Zones:** Environments that match what was broadcast on Chutti TV yesterday.
- **Tamil Voice Talent:** Fully localized with the original TV cast.

---

## 4. Business & Revenue Model
1. **Rewarded Video Ads (60%):** Watch to revive or double rewards—high conversion in India.
2. **Branded Integration (20%):** In-game billboards/power-ups for Chutti TV advertisers.
3. **Cosmetic IAP (20%):** Optional skins for characters and environments.

---

## 5. TV ↔ Game Synergy
- **Secret Codes:** TV show host reveals codes for in-game rewards.
- **Live Events:** Weekend challenges tied to "Mega Marathon" TV episodes.
- **QR Codes:** On-air end-cards driving instant downloads.
